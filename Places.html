Paseo Del Mar

Welcome to Paseo del Mar! This vibrant waterfront promenade is where locals and visitors alike come to relax by the sea. Enjoy a leisurely stroll, sample delicious snacks from local vendors, and soak in the beauty of Zamboanga's coastal charm. It's the perfect spot to unwind and enjoy the salty breeze.


Sta. Cruz Island

Santa Cruz Island in Zamboanga is renowned for its stunning pink sand beach, a natural wonder caused by microscopic red coral fragments mixing with white sand.

Abong-Abong

Welcome to Abong-Abong, a charming community nestled in the heart of Zamboanga! With its picturesque landscapes, vibrant culture, and warm hospitality, Abong-Abong invites you to experience the rich tapestry of Filipino life

Pasonanca Park

Welcome to Pasonangca Park, a serene oasis in Zamboanga! Ducks and other animals lazily paddle across the tranquil pond or simply wanders around. Admire the vibrant blooms in the flower gardens, or find a shady bench to lose yourself in a good book. Pasonangca Park welcomes you with open arms come and witness the beauty of Zamboanga's natural landscape.
